+ (MIKArrayOf(MIKMIDICommand *) commandsFromNoteEvent: (MIKMIDINoteEvent *)  noteEvent
clock: (nullable MIKMIDIClock *)  clock 

Creates an MIKMIDINoteOnCommand and MIKMIDINoteOffCommand from an MIKMIDINoteEvent.

Parameters
noteEventA MIKMIDINoteEvent instance.
clockAn MIKMIDIClock instance used to convert from the note event's sequence timestamp to a realtime MIDI time stamp. If clock is nil, the noteEvent's timestamp is ignored, and its duration is assumed to be a quarter note at the default MIDI tempo of 120 BPM.

Extends class MIKMIDICommand.
